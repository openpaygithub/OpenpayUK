  1. Your agreement with us

      in respect of any purchase (the “Contract”), you agree to pay Openpay Pty Ltd Pty Ltd ACN 159 699 126 of 40/120 Collins Street, Melbourne, Victoria 3000, Australia (“we”, “us”, “Openpay”) the instalments specified in the Openpay Deferred Payment Plan (the ” Plan”) contained in the store receipt that is issued to you in respect of that purchase.
    2. You agree to pay such instalments in consideration for Openpay paying the retailer the balance of the price for goods purchased by you from the retailer, and you agree to do so on these terms and conditions.
    3. You will be taken to have agreed to these terms and conditions and to the accompanying privacy terms contained in the section headed ‘Collection and use of your personal information’of this brochure by signing an electronic signature pad for the relevant Plan.
    4. By signing this Contract, you declare that all the information you have given in connection with it is accurate and not misleading and you are aware that we are relying on it.
    5. You acknowledge that Openpay may decline to enter this Contract with you in our absolute discretion.
    6. You must retain this Contract and your Plan as they represent your contract with Openpay.
  2. Your payment obligation
  3. This Contract is free from interest and free from upfront or monthly fees.
  4. You must pay a 20% deposit on your nominated MasterCard or Visa debit or credit card (“Nominated Card”) in store before you take your purchases home. If the purchase price of your goods exceeds the aggregate purchase amount threshold imposed by Openpay (details of which can be found on our website – myopenpay.co.nz) (the “Openpay Transaction Limit”), you must pay a 20% deposit on the Openpay Transaction Limit plus the additional amount that exceeds the Openpay Transaction Limit.
  5. You must pay the instalments set out in your Plan at the times specified in your Plan or earlier if you so choose.
  6. If you exercise a statutory or other right to obtain a refund of the purchase of any goods or services financed under this Contract, instalments which you have made to us will be refunded to your Nominated Card.
  • Payments – how made
    1. You must pay each instalment to us by its due date in full without set-off or deduction for any reason.
    2. Instalments may be paid by cheque or money order sent by post to GPO BOX 5262, Melbourne VIC 3001, Australia. Cheques and money orders must be made out to Openpay Pty Ltd Pty Ltd. You must indicate your name and Plan number when providing us with payment in this way.
    3. If we have not received your instalment by cheque or money order by the end of the repayment due date, or if a cheque or money order is dishonoured, you will be taken to have directed and authorised us to instead charge the payment to your Nominated Card.
    4. Payments may also be made by your Nominated Card. A processing fee of $2.50 will be charged at the time of payment for any payment by card (other than payment of the initial deposit which does not incur any fees). You must ensure there are sufficient funds available to be drawn from your Nominated Card account to allow any payment to be honoured.
    5. If we receive a cheque or money order from you in relation to an instalment after we have already charged your Nominated Card, we may elect to apply it towards your Plan or return it to you at our discretion.
    6. We may apply any payment you make in any manner we choose.
    7. We may apply any previously nominated card to any outstanding amounts under any of your Plans in the event that our attempt to process a payment on your Nominated Card is declined for any reason.
    8. Requested changes to payment schedules in your Plan may incur a Plan administration fee of $5 per variation and must be paid by you at the time of requesting the variation.
  • Default and its consequences
    1. You default under this Contract if:
      1. you fail to pay any money when due, or fail to comply with any of your other obligations under this Contract or your Plan ;
      2. you become bankrupt, are wound up or become subject to administration or receivership or any similar thing under any law;
      3. your Nominated Card is cancelled or suspended or our attempt to process a payment is declined for any reason; or
      4. we discover you gave us misleading or untrue information in relation to this Contract or your Plan .
    2. If you fail to make a payment by its due date, a late payment fee of $7.50 applies and must be paid by you immediately. You authorise us to recover this fee by a charge to your Nominated Card.
    3. If you default, we will advise you of this default and unless remedied within 7 business days of us giving you notice of the default, we may (in our sole discretion) require you to pay us the outstanding balance of all instalments owing under your Plan (even if they are not otherwise due) and all other money payable under this Contract including late payment fees and enforcement expenses we reasonably incur. In these circumstances, you will also be required to pay a Plan closing fee of $17.50 which must be paid by you at the time the Plan is closed.
