General

What is Openpay?

Openpay is a payment solution that enables customers to spread the cost of a purchase in equal monthly instalments. Pay over two or three months depending on the merchant with no hidden fees and no interest, ever.

Why should I use Openpay?

Openpay provides you with greater flexibility when you shop – with interest free monthly payment plans. Sign up with a few simple details and pay off your purchases in two or three months. It’s free to use, with no hidden fees or interest.

Can I use Openpay anywhere?

Founded in Australia in 2012, Openpay is now available in the UK. We are expanding our retail partners in the UK and Europe, so look out for our logo at checkout.

How do I use Openpay?

To create a new plan, select Openpay at the checkout with one of our merchant partners. You will be directed to either create an account or sign in, customise your plan and check your payment details.

Is Openpay a credit card?

Openpay is not a credit card or a bank, we do not offer credit or charge interest on your balance. The only thing you’ll need is a credit card (Visa or Mastercard) or a debit card . We use Continuous Payment Authority for your payments.

Are there any fees or interest?

There are no fees or interest, however if you miss a payment a late fee of £7.50 may be charged. Late charges are capped at £15 per plan.

Is my personal information secure with Openpay?

Absolutely. All sensitive information is encrypted, and we maintain stringent precautionary measures to protect your information. Our privacy policy can be viewed here.

How long are repayment lengths?

You have the option of a 2 or 3 month plan, with the initial instalment paid at the time of purchase.

How often do I need to pay?

Instalments are paid monthly and will be automatically charged to your nominated credit or debit card. You can customise your monthly payment date when you create a new plan.

Using Openpay

What do I need to use Openpay?

Our sign up process asks for a few things to qualify your application. You’ll need to be at least 18 years old and be a United Kingdom resident with a valid government issued photo ID.

We’ll also ask for your email and home address, the credit card (Visa/Mastercard ) or debit card you want payments to be taken from, and a phone number. All cards used need to be in your name. See our terms and conditions for more detail.

Do I need to pay anything upfront?

When you create a new plan, you will be asked to pay an initial instalment on that day. This will be either 25% or 33% of the total purchase amount, depending on your plan length.

How does Openpay verify me?

Openpay works with 3rd party suppliers to verify your ID. This is not a credit check and your privacy is protected.

Does Openpay perform a credit check?

Openpay performs a soft credit check on all new customers to validate your identity. These checks do not leave a mark on your credit file and are not visible to other lenders.

Is my information safe?

Yes. Security is a top priority for Openpay. All of your personal data is carefully protected and confidential, and we will never sell or misuse the information you’ve entrusted us with. For more information, you can read our ­privacy policy.

Payments

How do I make a payment?

During checkout, you can customise the date of your monthly instalments. On this date payment will be automatically charged to your nominated card.

If you want to make a payment before a scheduled payment due date, you can make a payment through your account or call customer support on 0800 048 9007 to process a payment.

How often can I make payments?

You can make manual payments as often as you like, but scheduled payments (what you chose when you set up your plan) will be monthly.

How do I update my card details?

You can update your card details through your Openpay account. If you’d rather talk to our team, you can contact us on 0800 048 9007 during business hours (Monday to Friday, 9am-5.30pm GMT).

If your card is lost or stolen, please update your details or get in touch to arrange a payment date that works.

NOTE: Please never email us your card details or leave them on our messaging service. But do leave us a message with your name and contact details and a customer service member will get back to you.

Can I pay my plan off early?

Yes, early payments can be made via your account or with our customer service team by calling customer support on 0800 048 9007. There is no charge for paying early.

What will my bank statement show?

Your bank statement will show Openpay UK Ltd. as the merchant of record.

When will payments show up as a transaction in my bank account ?

While we take payment on the date you choose, some banks can take 3-5 business days to process your funds. Your statement will reflect the date the funds clear, and in the interim your transaction could be shown as ‘pending’. If you’re unsure if your payment has cleared, check with your bank.

What if I need more time to make a payment?

We want to ensure that you’re able to make your repayments. Don’t wait until a payment fails, helping you with your budgeting is important to us. Reach out to us on 0800 048 9007 or via email to support@myopenpay.co.uk and we will discuss what we can do to assist you.

I missed a payment, what should I do?

Reach out to us on 0800 048 9007 or via email to support@myopenpay.co.uk and we will discuss what we can do to assist you.

Fees

Are there fees?

There are no fees or interest, however if you miss a payment a late charge of £7.50 may be incurred. Late charges are capped at £15 per plan.

How do you make money without charging fees?

We don’t charge customers any plan fees or interest. Our retailers pay a small fee for our services, however using Openpay as a customer won’t cost you any more than the price of your purchase.

Returns

What if I want to return my purchase?

Full returns and partial refunds are available with Openpay. Please return goods to the merchant from which they were purchased in accordance with the merchants returns policy. If your return is approved by the merchant, they will inform us. You will receive a full or partial refund of any amounts already paid. All returns are subject to the merchant’s return policy.

How do refunds work?

Any amounts paid by you will be refunded back onto your nominated card and any future instalments will be cancelled, or adjusted in the case of a partial refund.

If a merchant processed a refund, when do I see the funds in my account?

Openpay processes your refund as soon as we are notified by the retailer. Sometimes your bank may take 3-5 days to clear the funds back into your account.
