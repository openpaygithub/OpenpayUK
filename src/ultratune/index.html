How it works:

  • Download the Openpay app & create a new account.
  • Head into your local authorised Ultra Tune Auto Service Centre and mention that you’d like to pay for your service using Openpay.
  • During checkout, choose your first payment date and set your schedule on-the-spot. Payments can be made weekly or fortnightly to suit your budget and lifestyle!

What do you need to get started?

To create an Openpay account, you need to be an Australian/New Zealand resident & over 18 years old.
You’ll also need:

  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Photo ID
  • VISA or MasterCard (credit or debit)
  • Initial Installment: 20% of the purchase price
Things you should know
  • 1. An account creation fee for first time customers.
  • 2. A reoccurring payment fee which reflects the costs associated with maintaining the account.
  • Terms, conditions and lending criteria apply. Review the Openpay terms and conditions and fees before finalising your purchase by visiting the Openpay Website.
