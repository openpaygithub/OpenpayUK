How it works

1. Shop online

Look for the Openpay logo on your favourite online websites. Choose Openpay at the checkout.

2. Sign up

Get approved by Openpay in just 90 seconds with ID and a soft credit check.

3. Select your plan

Choose between a two or three month plan, and make your first payment today. The remainder will be debited from your chosen card in equal, monthly payments on the dates you choose. There’s no need to wait, your order will be shipped right away.

Pay on time and stay fee-free

The only fee we will ever charge is a late fee if you miss a payment.

Don’t worry, we’ll send you a notification to let you know when every automatic payment is scheduled to process.
