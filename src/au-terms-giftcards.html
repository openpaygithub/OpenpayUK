OPENPAY GIFT CARD SPECIAL OFFER TERMS & CONDITIONS (AHG – NIKE)

 

To be Eligible:

·      Customers must make a purchase at participating AHG dealerships to the value of $100 or more and pay with Openpay between 24 October 2018 and 24 January 2018.

·      Car purchases, alloy wheels and warranty products are excluded.

·      Only available at participating AHG dealerships in Western Australia.

·      One (1) Gift Card available per plan.

·      One (1) person cannot take out multiple plans on the same date.

 

To Redeem:

·      To redeem their Gift Card Customers must make a purchase at participating Western Australian Nike Stores to the value of $150 or more and pay with Openpay.

·      Gift Cards can only be redeemed In Store by presenting the physical card at participating Nike Stores in Western Australia. Gift Cards cannot be redeemed online.

·      Gift cards expire thirty (30) days after being issued to the customer.

·      Customers must use the entire $50 Gift card amount in the one purchase.

 

Other:

·      Gift Card’s can only be redeemed by the intended recipient. Openpay will not be responsible if Gift Cards have been lost or stolen. In the event of a lost or stolen card the customer will not be eligible to receive a new Gift Card.

·      Refunds will only be provided to the customer in accordance with Nike’s refund policy. In the event that a customer refunds an Openpay plan to which the $50 Gift card was orginally applied, the customer will only be refunded the amount actually paid to Openpay at the time of the refund. Customers will not be refunded the Gift Card or the $50 Gift Card amount.

·      The $50 Gift Card amount will be applied to the customers future payments to reduce the overall dollar ($) amount of each instalment. The $50 will not be deducted from the purchase price of the customers items and the initial deposit amount to be paid at checkout will be 20% of the full purchase price.
