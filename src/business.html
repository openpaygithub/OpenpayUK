Why Openpay?

We’re safe, responsible and transparent. Your customers are our customers, and your success is our success.

What can we do for you?

Increase basket size.

By offering your customers more time to pay, they’re able to spend more and plan their purchases. Use Openpay to up-sell and increase your AOV.

Increase customer loyalty.

Convert existing customers and attract new ones by offering the latest and greatest in consumer shopping trends. Allowing your customers the ease and convenience of paying over time will keep them coming back for more.

Provide a risk free, secure solution.

We assume all transactional risk, paying you in full the next business day. We mirror your return policies for partial and full refunds and we return our Openpay transaction fee to you.

What can we do for your
customers?

A smarter way to pay.

We provide a simple way for your customers to spread their payments and manage their finances responsibly without compromising on what they want.

Open & transparent transactions.

We are committed to providing your customers with clarity & transparency at every stage of their journey. We won’t ever charge them interest, payment or plan fees and everything is laid out clearly from the beginning and throughout.

Fulfilling orders, not fantasies.

Your customers always pay their first instalment at the time of purchase, with payments due at dates chosen by them. Why is this great? Your customers are able to truly budget, which means fewer regret purchases.
